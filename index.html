How to play?

WordWeaver is a trivia-powered crossword game. Use your knowledge to fill in the puzzle, one clue at a time.

  1. Start a Puzzle
    Tap Start Puzzle to begin.
  2. Choose a Difficulty
    Select Easy, Medium, or Hard to set the challenge level.
  3. Pick a Category
    Choose a topic like Science, History, or Pop Culture to customise your puzzle.
  4. Solve the Grid
    Use the clues to fill in the crossword. Select a box to type your letter.
